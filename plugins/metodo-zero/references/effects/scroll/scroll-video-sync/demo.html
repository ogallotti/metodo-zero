Scroll-Driven Storytelling

Instead of a traditional video file, this canvas draws a procedural animation scene that evolves with scroll. Particles form and dissolve, shapes orbit and transform, creating a narrative that unfolds at the user's own pace.

No Video Files Needed

Every frame is generated mathematically in real-time. This means zero loading time, infinite resolution, and the animation can respond to CSS custom properties for theming. Change the accent color and the entire animation adapts instantly.

Precise Control

The smoothing parameter controls how quickly the canvas catches up to the scroll position. Higher smoothing values create a more cinematic feel with momentum, while lower values give precise frame-by-frame control.

Versatile Application

Swap the procedural animation for any canvas-based visualization: data transitions, product reveals, diagram builds, or generative art. The scroll-sync engine is agnostic to what it renders.
