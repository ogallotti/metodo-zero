Procedural 3D Cube

No image assets required. The canvas generates a rotating 3D wireframe cube entirely through math. Scroll position maps directly to the rotation angle, creating a video-like scrub experience.

Smooth Interpolation

A lerp-based smoothing algorithm prevents jittery frame jumps. The canvas interpolates between the target frame and current frame at a configurable rate, producing buttery playback even at high scroll speeds.

Zero Dependencies

Pure canvas rendering with requestAnimationFrame. No external libraries, no image loading. The entire animation is generated mathematically, making it lightweight and instantly available without network requests.
