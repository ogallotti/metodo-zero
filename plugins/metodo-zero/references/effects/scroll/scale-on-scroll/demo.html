Scale on Scroll

Elements scale up as they approach the center of the viewport and shrink as they move away. Creates a natural focus-and-blur reading experience.

Think big.

Immersive interfaces

When elements grow as they enter focus, users feel pulled into the content. Scale creates implied depth on a flat screen.

Guided attention

The scale effect naturally draws the eye to the center of the viewport, creating a reading flow that requires zero conscious effort from the user.

Build fast.

Performance matters

Only transform and opacity are animated — the two properties browsers can composite on the GPU without triggering layout recalculation.

Ship with confidence

Reduced motion support ensures accessibility. The effect degrades gracefully to static elements when the user prefers less motion.
