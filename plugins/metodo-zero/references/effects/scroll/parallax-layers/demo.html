Parallax Layers

Multiple layers move at different speeds as you scroll, creating a sense of depth and visual richness.

Depth through motion

Background elements drift slowly while foreground content stays anchored. The differential speed tricks your brain into perceiving three-dimensional space.

Performance optimized

Only transform properties are animated, keeping compositing on the GPU. Passive scroll listeners and requestAnimationFrame ensure zero jank.

Fully customizable

Adjust layer speeds via CSS custom properties. The background and midground layers each have their own speed multiplier for fine-tuned control.
