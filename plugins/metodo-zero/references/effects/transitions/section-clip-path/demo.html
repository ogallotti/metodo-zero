Section Clip Path

Sections transition with geometric clip-path shapes — arrows, scoops, slashes, and diamonds — creating seamless visual connections between content blocks.

Scoop transition

An elliptical scoop at the bottom of this section reveals the next one beneath, creating an organic curved handoff between backgrounds.

The ellipse shape works especially well for hero-to-content transitions.

Angular slash

A diagonal cut at the section boundary adds energy and forward momentum. The angle draws the eye downward, encouraging continued scrolling.

Adjust the overlap with the --scp-overlap variable.

Diamond notch

A geometric diamond shape punctuates the transition between sections. The angular cutout creates a distinctive, modern separation.

This shape pairs well with bold color contrasts between sections.

Arrow revisited

The same arrow shape from the hero, now with different colors. Clip-path transitions are infinitely reusable across different section themes.

Mix and match shapes throughout your page for visual variety.
