Color Wipe

A solid color sweeps across the viewport to transition between sections. Three directional variants: left-to-right, right-to-left, and bottom-to-top.

Left to right

The wipe enters from the left, covers the full section, then exits to the right — revealing the new content beneath. The scaleX animation keeps it performant.

Uses cubic-bezier easing for a satisfying acceleration curve.

Right to left

Same technique, reversed direction. Alternating wipe directions between sections creates visual rhythm and prevents the transition from feeling repetitive.

Custom wipe color via inline style or --wipe-color variable.

Bottom to top

The vertical variant sweeps upward, aligning with the natural scroll direction. This creates a curtain-rising feeling that complements the downward scroll motion.

One-shot triggers

Each wipe fires once when its section enters the viewport. The observer disconnects after triggering, so scrolling back up won't replay the animation. Clean and intentional.
