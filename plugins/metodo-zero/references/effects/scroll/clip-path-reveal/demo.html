Clip Path Reveal

Sections reveal through animated clip-path shapes as you scroll. Three reveal variants: circle, inset, and polygon.

Circle reveal

A circular mask expands from the center of the section, gradually revealing the background behind it. The content fades in once the reveal passes the midpoint.

Inset reveal

The section reveals from bottom to top using an inset clip-path. This creates a curtain-like rising effect that feels natural with vertical scroll direction.

Polygon reveal

A triangular polygon expands upward and outward, creating a dramatic V-shaped unveiling effect. The geometric shape adds visual energy to the transition.

Combine freely

Mix and match reveal shapes across sections. Each variant adds a different character — circular for organic feels, inset for clean lines, polygon for dynamic energy.
