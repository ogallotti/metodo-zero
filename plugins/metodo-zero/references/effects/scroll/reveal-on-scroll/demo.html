Reveal on Scroll

Elements animate into view as you scroll, each with its own configurable animation type.

Slide Up

Content glides upward from below, the most natural and popular scroll-reveal pattern. It creates a sense of content emerging from beneath the fold, guiding the eye downward through the page.

Slide from Left

Horizontal reveals create a different energy, suggesting lateral movement and progression. They work especially well for timeline-style content or feature comparisons that flow from one side to the other.

Scale In

Elements that scale from small to full size create a zoom-like effect, drawing the eye to the center of each element. This pattern works beautifully for hero images, icons, and call-to-action buttons.

Rotate In

A subtle rotation combined with opacity gives elements a playful entrance. Best used sparingly for accent elements that need to stand out from the rest of the page content.

Mix and Match

Use `data-reveal` attributes to assign any animation type per element. Combine with `data-reveal-stagger` for sequenced entrances and `data-reveal-delay` for custom timing.
