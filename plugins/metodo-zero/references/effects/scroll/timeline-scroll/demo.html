Timeline Scroll

A timeline that draws itself as you scroll, with nodes and content cards appearing at each milestone.

Phase 1

Research & Discovery

Deep user research uncovers the core problems worth solving. We interview stakeholders, analyze competitors, and map the opportunity landscape to build a foundation of understanding.

Phase 2

Architecture & Prototyping

Technical architecture decisions shape everything that follows. We prototype the riskiest assumptions first, validating feasibility before committing to a full implementation path.

Phase 3

Design System Foundation

A robust design system ensures visual consistency across every screen. Tokens, components, and patterns are documented and stress-tested before scaling to the full product surface.

Phase 4

Development Sprint

Two-week sprints with daily standups keep momentum high. Each iteration ships a testable increment, gathering real feedback that feeds directly into the next cycle of improvement.

Phase 5

Launch & Iterate

Launch is the beginning, not the end. Telemetry and user sessions reveal the real usage patterns. We iterate rapidly, refining performance, accessibility, and delight with every release.

Scroll-Driven Storytelling

The timeline line fills as you scroll. Nodes light up and cards animate in as each milestone enters the viewport. Pure vanilla JS with IntersectionObserver.
