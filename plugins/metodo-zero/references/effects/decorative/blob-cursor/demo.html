Blob Cursor

A large soft gradient blob follows your cursor with smooth elastic easing, creating a liquid ambient glow behind your content.

Elastic Motion

The blob uses interpolation-based easing for a natural, organic feel that responds fluidly to cursor movement.

Screen Blend

Uses mix-blend-mode: screen for additive light blending that naturally interacts with your dark background.

GPU Accelerated

Transform and filter operations leverage GPU compositing for smooth 60fps animation without layout thrashing.

Configurable

Size, blur, colors, opacity, and easing are all adjustable via CSS custom properties and JS parameters.
